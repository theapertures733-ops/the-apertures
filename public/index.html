The Author

Written in the Dark

I write for the readers who have always felt that romance deserved more — more heat, more honesty, more of the things that people think in the dark but rarely say out loud.

Every story in The Apertures collection is built like a film. There is a mood before there is a plot. There is tension before there is touch. I believe in the slow build — the kind that makes the release feel earned.

These are not love stories. They are desire stories. And there is a difference.

— The Apertures
